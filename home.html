The French Capital of Street Art

Vitry-sur-Seine:
A Dynamic Cultural Hub Near Paris

Located just south of Paris, Vitry-sur-Seine is a vibrant city known for its rich artistic scene and dynamic urban landscape. As a leading hub for street art in France, the city boasts an open-air gallery of large-scale murals, making every walk a discovery.

Beautiful structures to visit

Vitry-sur-Seine offers a mix of art, culture, and nature. The MAC VAL is a highlight, showcasing contemporary art in a dynamic setting. Street art lovers can explore the city’s open-air murals, created by renowned artists. For a relaxing break, Parc des Lilas provides vast green spaces and scenic walking trails. History enthusiasts can visit Église Saint-Germain, a historic church with centuries of heritage. With its blend of creativity and tranquility, Vitry-sur-Seine has something for every visitor.
