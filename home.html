The French Capital of Street Art

Vitry-sur-Seine:
A Dynamic Cultural Hub Near Paris

Located just south of Paris, Vitry-sur-Seine is a vibrant city known for its rich artistic scene and dynamic urban landscape. As a leading hub for street art in France, the city boasts an open-air gallery of large-scale murals, making every walk a discovery.

Beautiful places to visit

Vitry-sur-Seine is a charming city, blending green spaces, fascinating museums, and stunning views of the Seine, only 10 minutes away from Paris. Its unique atmosphere offers the perfect mix of modernity and tradition. History enthusiasts can visit Église Saint-Germain, a historic church with centuries of heritage, while others will enjoy its parks and museums. Vitry-sur-Seine has something for every visitor. Get ready to explore this vibrant city!

A town full of history

Vitry-sur-Seine started as a medieval rural village during the medieval period. It grew rapidly in th 19th century because of industrialization and the development of railways. The 20th century saw urgan growth and a rise in immigration. Today, it is a very diverse city where traces of its past are still visible. From the fallen castle to the Victims of War memorial, the city’s past is woven into its streets.
